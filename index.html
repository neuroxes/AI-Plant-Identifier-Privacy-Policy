    4. Data Storage and Retention

    Cloud Storage (Firebase)

    Account profiles, subscription data, and credit balances are stored in Firebase Realtime Database. This data persists until you delete your account.

    Local Device Storage

    Plant libraries, care routines, and AI Coach chat histories are cached locally using Hive and SharedPreferences. This data is deleted when you uninstall the app or clear app data.

    Temporary Processing

    Plant images sent to OpenAI for identification are processed transiently and not permanently stored by the AI service.

    Retention Period

    We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide services or as required by law. Deleted accounts are purged within 30 days.

    6. Security Measures

    We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your information:

    • Encrypted Communications: All data transfers use HTTPS/TLS encryption
    • Firebase Security Rules: Database access is restricted to authenticated users' own data nodes
    • API Key Protection: Sensitive keys are stored outside the codebase and loaded securely at runtime
    • Sanitized Identifiers: Device IDs are processed to remove sensitive hardware information
    • Regular Updates: We maintain current security patches for all dependencies

    Important: While we take reasonable precautions, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. Please use strong passwords and keep your account credentials confidential.
